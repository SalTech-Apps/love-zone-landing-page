Our Commitment to Child Safety

At AgapeMate, we are deeply committed to creating a safe and secure environment for all our users. As a Christian-based platform, we uphold the highest standards of integrity, love, and care, especially when it comes to protecting children. We have implemented robust measures to ensure that our app is not accessible to individuals under the age of 18.

Age Restrictions

Our app is designed exclusively for individuals who are 18 years of age or older. During the registration process, we require users to confirm their age. Any account found to be created by someone under the age of 18 will be immediately terminated. We also use advanced age verification tools to ensure compliance.

Reporting and Moderation

We have a dedicated team of moderators who actively monitor the app for inappropriate behavior or content. Users can easily report any suspicious activity or content through our in-app reporting system. All reports are taken seriously and investigated promptly. We encourage all users to be vigilant and report any concerns regarding child safety.

Parental Controls and Guidance

We strongly recommend that parents and guardians take an active role in their children's online activities. If you believe your child has created an account on our app, please contact us immediately at agapemate@saltechapps.com. We will take swift action to remove the account and ensure the safety of your child.

Education and Awareness

We believe that education is the cornerstone of online safety. To support parents and guardians, we provide resources and tips on how to talk to children about online safety, the importance of age-appropriate behavior, and how to recognize potential risks. Together, we can create a safer online environment for everyone.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or need assistance regarding child safety on our platform, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you. You can contact us at agapemate@saltechapps.com.
